About


Tsukasa Kimura (b. 2000, Tokyo) is a Multidisciplinary Artist in London, UK originally from Tokyo.

Working organically in the digital medium, the artist explores and attempts to give autonomy to the crucial role of imagination in the construction of collective historical memories.
